## Class Data
- Description: Armored champion combining martial strength with holy power and defense.
- Stat Changes: +2 STR, +1 CHA, -1 DEX
- Specialty: Holy Resolve — Gain +2 against fear and corruption effects, and allies within 5 ft gain +1 on morale-related saves.
- Skills:

  - Aura of Courage — Allies within 5 ft cannot be frightened for 2 turns. Cost: 5 Mana. Cooldown: 4 turns.
  - Judgment Blade — Melee hit deals 2d6 radiant damage and silences cursed/undead targets for 1 turn. Cost: 7 Mana. Cooldown: 4 turns.
- Skill Unlock Requirements:
  - Smite: STR 12+, CHA 12+
  - Lay on Hands: STR 13+, CHA 14+
  - Aura of Courage: STR 15+, CHA 15+
  - Judgment Blade: STR 17+, CHA 16+, holy weapon equipped
