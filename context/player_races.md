# Player Races

Information regarding player(s) numerical stats values, definitions, purposes, and format.

## Definition & Display Format
| Race | Description | Stat Changes | Specialty |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| **Human** | Versatile and adaptable, able to survive in nearly any role without excelling too narrowly. | <span style="color:green">+1 to any 2 stats</span> | **Adaptability** — Once per long rest, choose one skill check or saving throw after rolling and gain **+2** to the result. |
| **Dwarf** | Hardy and resilient, built for survival, labor, and close combat in harsh environments. | <span style="color:green">+2 CON</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 STR</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 DEX</span> | **Endurance** — Gain **+2** on checks or saves against poison, exhaustion, and forced-march fatigue. Reduce incoming physical damage by **1** once per combat round. |
| **Orc** | Fierce and physically dominant, known for raw power and intimidation in battle. | <span style="color:green">+2 STR</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 CON</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 CHA</span> | **Ferocity** — When below **50% HP**, gain **+2 damage** on melee attacks. Once per combat, gain **+2** on an Intimidation or melee attack roll. |
| **Elf** | Agile and perceptive, with natural grace, precision, and heightened awareness. | <span style="color:green">+2 DEX</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 INT</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 CON</span> | **Keen Senses** — Gain **+2 Perception** and **+1 Initiative**. Hidden enemies have their detection difficulty reduced by **2** for you. |
| **Draconian** | Dragon-blooded and imposing, carrying ancient power and commanding presence. | <span style="color:green">+2 CHA</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 STR</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 DEX</span> | **Draconic Presence** — Once per short rest, force nearby enemies within **10 ft** to make a morale check at **-2** or become shaken for **1 turn**. |
| **Halfling** | Small, quick, and surprisingly brave, often surviving through luck and agility. | <span style="color:green">+2 DEX</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 CHA</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 STR</span> | **Luck** — Once per long rest, reroll **1** failed check, save, or attack roll. You must keep the new result. |
| **Gnome** | Clever and curious, gifted in invention, trickery, and magical study. | <span style="color:green">+2 INT</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 DEX</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 STR</span> | **Ingenuity** — Gain **+2** on puzzle-solving, trap-disarming, and invention-related checks. Crafting or analysis tasks take **25% less time** when relevant. |
| **Tiefling** | Marked by infernal blood, naturally persuasive but often mistrusted. | <span style="color:green">+2 CHA</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 INT</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 WIS</span> | **Hellborn Influence** — Gain **+2 Persuasion/Deception**, but suffer **-1** on first-impression social checks with lawful or religious NPCs. |
| **Goblin** | Fast, sneaky, and opportunistic, but physically weak and poorly received. | <span style="color:green">+2 DEX</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 WIS</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 STR</span> | **Sneakcraft** — Gain **+2 Stealth** and deal **+1d4** bonus damage when attacking a target that is unaware, distracted, or already engaged. |
| **Giantkin** | Massive and durable people with overwhelming strength but limited speed. | <span style="color:green">+2 STR</span><br><span style="color:green">+2 CON</span><br><span style="color:red">-2 DEX</span> | **Colossus Build** — Gain **+2** on grapples, shoves, and carrying checks. You may wield heavy weapons without penalty, but suffer **-1 Initiative**. |
| **Fae** | Supernatural beings tied to magic and trickery, but frail in direct conflict. | <span style="color:green">+2 CHA</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 DEX</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 CON</span> | **Glamour** — Once per short rest, gain **+3** on one charm, deception, or distraction attempt. Non-hostile creatures resist your social influence at **-1**. |
| **Undead** | Reanimated or death-touched beings, difficult to break but unsettling to others. | <span style="color:green">+2 CON</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 WIS</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 CHA</span> | **Deathless** — Gain **+2** against fear, poison, and pain-based effects. You do not need to eat or sleep normally, but healing from normal rations/rest is reduced by **50%**. |
| **Beastfolk** | Animal-featured humanoids guided by instinct, speed, and sharpened senses. | <span style="color:green">+2 DEX</span><br><span style="color:green">+1 WIS</span><br><span style="color:red">-1 INT</span> | **Primal Instinct** — Gain **+2 Survival** and **+2 Tracking**. You cannot be surprised except by enemies whose stealth result exceeds your perception by **5+**. |
